# Quantum Computing: A Developer's Guide

Quantum computing represents a paradigm shift in computational power. This guide explores the fundamentals and practical applications for developers.

## Understanding Quantum Principles

### 1. Superposition
The ability of quantum bits to exist in multiple states simultaneously.

### 2. Entanglement
The phenomenon where quantum particles become correlated and share states.

### 3. Quantum Gates
Basic building blocks of quantum circuits.

## Programming Quantum Computers

```python
# Example quantum circuit using Qiskit
from qiskit import QuantumCircuit, execute, Aero
qc = QuantumCircuit(2, 2)
qc.h(0)
qc.cx(0, 1)
qc.measure([0,1], [0,1])
```
